Tartiflette

A tasty french dish for cheese lovers.

Ingredients

  • 750g potatoes
  • 150g bacon
  • 1 oignon
  • 1 glass of white wine>
  • Cream
  • 1 reblochon

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 180°C fan/200°C/400°F/ gas mark 6.
  2. Peel then cut the potatoes. Simmer them in a saucepan of lightly salted water until just cooked
  3. Fry the lardons
  4. transfer the potatoes to a gratin dish big enough to fit all the potatoes without them spilling out the top. Pour over the onion/bacon mixture and mix a bit. Slice the cheese with the rind on and lay over the top of the gratin.
  5. Bake in the oven for 15–20 minutes, until the cheese has browned and melted fully
