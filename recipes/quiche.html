Quiche Lorraine

Ingredients

  • 1 pie crust
  • 4 eggs
  • crème fraiche
  • milk
  • bacon
  • Swiss cheese

Steps

  1. Fry the bacon
  2. In a mixing bowl, whisk together the eggs, the crème fraiche, milk, bacon and cheese
  3. Pour the prepared liquid over the crust
  4. Bake for about 45-55 minutes
